ABOUT US

We Build the Web. We Build Futures.

Since 2014, Hot Beans Web has been crafting high‑quality digital experiences for businesses of all sizes — while helping new developers grow into confident professionals.

OUR STORY

Hot Beans Web began as a two‑person passion project created by Alex Turner and Maya Singh, two developers who believed the industry needed more honesty, better code, and real opportunities for new talent.

What started in a rented office above a café has grown into a thriving studio with a 40‑person team, delivering over 250 digital products for clients across the UK and Europe.

Our mission has always been simple: build digital experiences that are fast, accessible, and meaningful — and support the next generation of developers who share that vision.

OUR MISSION

  • Performance First — Every site we build is optimised for speed, stability, and long‑term scalability.
  • Accessibility Always — We follow WCAG standards to ensure every user can access our work.
  • Grow Together — Many of our senior developers started as trainees right here at Hot Beans.

OUR VALUES

What Drives Us Forward

Our values aren’t slogans — they guide every decision, every project, and every interaction.

Quality First

We prioritise building things the right way. Clean code, accessibility, and performance are non‑negotiable parts of our workflow.

Open Communication

We believe in honesty at every level. Team updates, project goals, and company progress are shared openly so everyone stays aligned.

Growth Mindset

Every team member receives dedicated learning time and resources. We push each other to improve, experiment, and stay curious.

Diversity & Respect

We welcome people from all backgrounds. Our hiring and culture focus on fairness, representation, and creating a safe environment for everyone.

Eco‑Friendly Development

We optimise every project for low energy usage and minimal data transfer. Better for users, better for the planet.

Constructive Feedback

Regular check‑ins, open discussions, and anonymous suggestions help us improve as a team and as individuals.
